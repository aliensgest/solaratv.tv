Installation Guides

Amazon Fire Stick

  1. Go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options
  2. Enable "Apps from Unknown Sources"
  3. Search for "Downloader" in the Amazon App Store and install it
  4. Open Downloader and enter the app URL we provide via WhatsApp
  5. Install the IPTV app and open it
  6. Enter your SOLARA TV credentials (M3U URL or Xtream Codes)
  7. Wait for channels to load and enjoy!
💡 Tip: Use an Ethernet adapter or 5GHz Wi-Fi for the best 4K streaming experience.

Android TV / Box

  1. Open Google Play Store on your Android TV
  2. Search for "TiviMate" or "IPTV Smarters Pro"
  3. Install and open the app
  4. Select "Xtream Codes API" login method
  5. Enter your SOLARA TV credentials
  6. Configure EPG and enjoy!
💡 Tip: TiviMate offers the best user interface for Android TV.

iOS (iPhone / iPad)

  1. Open the App Store
  2. Search for "IPTV Smarters Player"
  3. Install and open the app
  4. Tap "Login with Xtream Codes API"
  5. Enter your SOLARA TV credentials
  6. Start streaming!
💡 Tip: You can also use VLC for iOS with an M3U playlist.

Windows / Mac

  1. Download VLC Media Player from videolan.org
  2. Open VLC → Media → Open Network Stream
  3. Paste your M3U URL provided by SOLARA TV
  4. Click Play and enjoy!

Alternatively, use IPTV Smarters Pro for Windows for a better experience with EPG guide.

💡 Tip: Use Kodi with PVR IPTV Simple Client add-on for the most customizable experience.

Samsung / LG Smart TV

  1. Open your TV's App Store (Samsung: Smart Hub, LG: LG Content Store)
  2. Search for "IPTV Smarters" or "Smart IPTV"
  3. Install and open the app
  4. Enter your SOLARA TV credentials
  5. Wait for channels to load and enjoy!
💡 Tip: If the app isn't available, use a Fire Stick plugged into your TV's HDMI port for the best experience.
