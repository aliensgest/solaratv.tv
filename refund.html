14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We're confident you'll love SOLARA TV. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment within 14 days of purchase — no questions asked.

How to Request a Refund

1

Contact Us

Reach out via WhatsApp or email within 14 days of purchase

2

Provide Details

Share your account email and reason for the refund

3

Receive Refund

We'll process your refund within 3–5 business days

Eligibility

  • Refund requests must be made within 14 days of the original purchase date
  • Applies to all subscription plans (1-month, 3-month, 6-month, and 12-month)
  • Only the first purchase per customer is eligible for a full refund
  • Renewal payments are eligible if requested within 14 days of the renewal date

Non-Refundable Cases

  • Requests made after the 14-day guarantee period
  • Accounts terminated due to violation of our Terms of Service
  • Chargebacks initiated without contacting our support team first
  • Second or subsequent refund requests from the same customer

Refund Processing

Once approved, refunds are processed as follows:

  • Credit/Debit Card: 3–5 business days to appear on your statement
  • PayPal: 1–3 business days
  • Cryptocurrency: Refunded in equivalent value at time of processing

All refunds are issued to the original payment method used at the time of purchase.

Plan Upgrades & Downgrades

If you upgrade your plan, the price difference is non-refundable. However, you may request a full refund of the upgraded plan within 14 days of the upgrade, in which case your account will revert to the previous plan.

Contact for Refunds

Our support team is available 24/7 and typically responds within 1 hour.
