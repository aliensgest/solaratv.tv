Reseller Program FAQ

What is 1 credit?
1 credit = 1 month of SOLARA TV service. Use credits to create 1‑month, 3‑month, 6‑month or 12‑month subscriptions for your customers.
Do credits expire?
No. Your credits never expire – you can use them anytime, even after years.
What happens if I don't use all my credits immediately?
Your credits never expire. Whether you sell them all in one month or spread them over two years, every credit stays valid. No pressure, no deadlines – you control your business pace.
Can I sell subscriptions to multiple customers with one credit package?
Absolutely. Each credit = 1 month for 1 customer. With 240 credits, you can serve 240 different customers for 1 month each, or 20 customers for 12 months each. One package, unlimited possibilities.
What support do resellers receive?
Dedicated 24/7 WhatsApp & Telegram support, marketing materials, and a personal account manager for 240+ credits.
Which regions can I sell to?
Europe, USA, Canada, Australia, GCC (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain) + 30+ countries. Our servers are optimized for each region.
What payment methods do you accept?
Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, iDEAL, Bancontact, and SEPA Bank Transfer.
